The interesting thing about the dithering algorithm, is its camouflaging attitude. What do I mean? Good dithering algorithms, when properly developed, should remain unnoticed by the viewers eyes. Curiously enough when the same algorithm is applied to gradients its camouflaging qualities get weaker revealing the algorithm structure in the form of colorful patterns. Thee additional control on the quantization error algortihm allows for more in depth analysis of the dithering process.

Despite the visual result of the exploitation of the latter algorithm, my interests lies also in the revelatory quality of the project exposing the algorithm as himself.

FISIOGNOMIC HOROSCOPE

The Fisiognomic Horoscope was created for the Yami-Ichi Flea Market @H3K. Thanks to Wekinator, a machine learning software, it was possible to train a model with celebrities faces, and their astrological sign. People can sit in front of the camera and let their face be scanned. The trained model matches their face traits with the ones in the datatbase. At the end the matching horoscope is printed. The project was done to bring awareness about the use of machine learning in the facial recognition field. Some Ph.D students already applied machine lerning to determine if someone could be a criminal or not. This was possible by training a model with the portrait of already convicted criminal.

This research leads back to the time were Physiognomy and Phrenology were considered real sciences, despite dicovering later on that they were not.


I am currently trying to port this project to JavaScript with tensorFlow.js.

SELBERSCHULD!

In the German language there is an expression selberschuld, that translated in English means: you have to blame yourself.

Selberschuld is the non-solidarity practice, we use it primarily to distance ourselves from problems someone else has.

In this series of posters I use this expression to describe the disgrace of the young unemployed, the migrants that die traveling to Europe and the heroin addiction related deaths in Switzerland.

The expression is repeatedly used to design a line diagram, in which the darker letter draw the statistics. Those three examples extremely represent how far we can go with our non-solidarity practice. Incapable to help, we rather decide that the guilt of their disgrace is only upon them, relieving us from being empathic. (that in my understanding means: I really don‚Äôt care about your problems let me live my life without being involved in yours)
